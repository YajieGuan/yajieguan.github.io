Senior Hardware Systems Integration, Validation and Test Engineer with 7+ years of experience in complex electro-optical and mixed-signal hardware systems. Expertise in system validation, integration, and debugging of hardware platforms spanning electronics and embedded systems, with a PhD in precision measurement systems including laser stabilization, interferometry, and low-noise sensing.

Strong background in system-level integration across optics, electronics, and embedded systems,
with emphasis on calibration and performance validation. Experienced in validating and debugging of early-stage experimental hardware under incomplete specifications, with a proven ability to identify and resolve issues across hardware, firmware, and instrumentation layers using lab equipment and data-driven analysis to improve system reliability and performance.

Hands-on experience developing automated test, calibration, and validation infrastructure for both R\&D and production environments. Demonstrated effective cross-functional collaboration across electrical, firmware, optics, and manufacturing teams to deliver robust, scalable hardware systems.
